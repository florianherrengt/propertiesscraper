Full description

It is with immense excitement and pleasure that we have the opportunity to offer for sale this completely unique and bespoke residence situated within the exceptionally sought after setting of the 'The Court' at Marine Gate Mansions Southport.

For buyers wishing to find an impressive, individual yet spacious abode, they should look no further. This stunning property presented over three floors is briefly comprising of a secluded and private entrance which leads you to the 1st floor where you enter an extensive open plan reception lounge and dining area with feature vaulted ceiling above. Fabulous unrestricted views are provided from the west-facing lounge windows where at your leisure you can watch the picturesque sunsets over the Marine Lake and the Irish Sea beyond.

Continuing on the 1st floor, there is a recently fitted breakfast kitchen master bedroom with an exterior balcony and en suite bathroom, 2nd double bedroom with en suite shower room, separate cloakroom/WC, and spiral staircase to the 2nd floor.

The 2nd floor offers a glass panelled gallery landing which looks down onto the reception lounge below. A small set of steps provides access into a vaulted ceiling double bedroom which has been styled to provide our client with their very own fully fitted home office. The 4th bedroom is situated along the landing and again has its very own en suite shower room.

The property is located within a handsome courtyard setting of similar properties with manicured gardens, stone pathways, and access to the underground parking garage.
Viewing is an absolute must for the discerning property buyer wanting a residence like no other.
Please call Bailey Estates today for further information and to arrange an early viewing.

Traveling north on Lord Street continue to the fire station roundabout where you will take the first exit into Leicester Street. Continue until the next roundabout where you will take the first exit to the Promenade and 1st left again into the Marine Gate Mansions Complex. 'The Court' is situated on the right-hand side.


Entrance Hallway 
10' 3'' x 3' 4'' (3.13m x 1.03m)
An inviting entrance hallway with a panelled radiator presented to the sidewall. Alarm keypad panel situated to the side. Stairs rise up to the first-floor accommodation.

Open Hall 
8' 8'' x 6' 8'' (2.65m x 2.05m)
Stairs rise from the ground to the first-floor open hall. Glass panelled banister rail, recessed lighting to ceiling and side door which opens into a separate cloak/WC, and also airing cupboard which houses the water tank.

Cloak/WC 
5' 11'' x 3' 1'' (1.82m x 0.94m)
Separate Cloak/WC with bespoke Amtico floor laid throughout, wall mounted heated towel rail and recessed lighting to ceiling. The suite is comprising of a low-level dual flush WC and modern wall mounted glass bowl washbasin.

Breakfast Kitchen 
19' 5'' x 9' 8'' (5.93m x 2.95m)
Recently renovated and bespoke fitted breakfast kitchen. Amtico flooring laid throughout, modern vertical mounted radiator and twin glazed side windows. Attractive range of base and eye level units fitted throughout with a striking granite work surface. Integrated appliances are comprising of a 'Bosch' induction hob, extractor, double ovens, and a 'Neff' dishwasher. There is ample space and services in place for additional stand-alone electrical appliances. Within the side cupboard, a Viessman GCH boiler is fitted at eye level.

Reception Lounge & Diner 
30' 2'' x 21' 11'' (9.2m x 6.7m)
An outstanding feature of the apartment is the large open plan reception lounge and dining area with picture windows presented to the side and rear aspect providing stunning views over well-maintained gardens, Southport Marine Lake, and the Irish sea beyond. Within the room itself there are three bespoke vertical mounted panelled radiators, and to the side exterior wall is an impressive coal effect living flame gas fire. A feature spiral staircase within leads up to the second floor where a vaulted ceiling rises to the apex of the apartment.

Master Bedroom 
22' 1'' x 9' 10'' (6.75m x 3.01m)
Excellent master bedroom with French doors to the front aspect that step out onto an exterior tiled paved terrace balcony with wrought iron railings offering views over to the courtyard beyond. To the rear of the bedroom, there are double glazed picture windows that provide perfect views onto Southport Marine Lake and onward to the Irish sea. Bespoke vertical mounted tubular radiator presented to the front aspect. Interior side door opens into an en suite bathroom.

En Suite Bathroom 
9' 10'' x 6' 4'' (3m x 1.95m)
Luxury En suite bathroom with Amtico floor laid throughout. Recessed lighting to the ceiling and a wall mounted heated towel rail. Fully tiled walls and a suite comprising of a large double Jacuzzi spa bath, separate power shower with overhead and wall-mounted jets, pedestal sink and a low-level flush WC.

Bedroom 2 
14' 6'' x 10' 11'' (4.45m x 3.35m)
First-floor double bedroom with double glazed side windows which look out onto the neighbouring Marine Gate Mansions. Panelled radiator presented to the sidewall and there are a full suite of floor to ceiling wall to wall wardrobes and a dressing table. An interior door enters into an en suite shower room.

En Suite Shower Room 
7' 6'' x 4' 5'' (2.31m x 1.37m)
En Suite shower room with fully tiled floor and partially tiled walls. Recessed lighting fitted to the ceiling, and a low-level wall mounted heated towel rail. The bathroom suite is comprising of a pedestal sink, low level flush WC and a separate enclosed shower.

Gallery Landing 
25' 7'' x 18' 2'' (7.81m x 5.54m)
(measurements include open space over the 1st floor below) A modern spiral staircase provides access from the first to the second-floor gallery landing with a modern glass panelled banister which provides views to the room below. A high-level vaulted ceiling with recessed lighting rises to the Apex of the roof. Panelled radiator presented to the sidewall, and there is a low-level mirror sliding door cloak cupboard.

Bedroom 3 
15' 3'' x 14' 1'' (4.67m x 4.31m)
Third double bedroom situated to the second floor with a vaulted ceiling housing two skylight windows and recessed lighting. The bedroom benefits from a selection of floor to ceiling fitted wardrobes, dressing table, chest of drawers and bedside tables. A panelled radiator is fitted below the two windows. In addition, there is ample under eave storage, and an internal door which provides access into an en suite shower room.

En Suite Shower Room 
6' 1'' x 5' 6'' (1.87m x 1.68m)
En suite shower room with an Amtico floor laid throughout, and partially tiled walls and recessed lighting to ceiling. A modern feature of the en suite shower room is the frosted glass block wall. The suite is comprising of a separate shower, pedestal sink, and low-level flush WC. Wall-mounted heated towel rail.

Bedroom 4 
13' 1'' x 12' 3'' (4m x 3.75m)
Stepping up from the gallery landing to a double bedroom with twin skylight windows and recessed lighting presented within the vaulted ceiling. A panelled radiator is mounted below the windows, and there is additional under eave storage to the side. This double bedroom is presently utilised by our clients as a home office/study and has a full suite of fully fitted desks, shelving, drawers and storage units fitted within.

Exterior 
The property is situated in one of the most attractive courtyard settings that Southport has to offer. 'The Court' situated adjacent to Marine Gate Mansions hosts a vast array of shrubs, plants, bushes and trees throughout with a centre communal grass laid to lawn garden and stone pathway which leads around the perimeter and onward to our clients beautiful home. To the front aspect is an extensive expanse of grass laid to lawn communal gardens with access also to the secure garage below.
